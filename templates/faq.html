Step-by-step guide:
  1. Select Data: Choose "Some of your information" and check "Followers and following" under "Connections" tab
  2. Choose Format: Click "Download to device" then select either "JSON" or "HTML" format with date range of your choice (for best results, use all time)
  3. Wait for Instagram to email you the download link (usually takes a few hours)
  4. Download and extract the zip file
  5. Look for followers_1.json/html and following.json/html files
Tip: The download process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to 48 hours depending on Instagram's current load.

Your Privacy Matters:
  • All processing happens in your browser
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • Files are deleted immediately after processing
  • No personal information is collected
100% Private
Your Instagram data never leaves your device except for the brief moment it's processed to show you results.

Supported Formats:
JSON followers_1.json, following.json
HTML followers_1.html, following.html
File Size Limit: Maximum 50MB per file

Non-followers are accounts that you follow, but they don't follow you back. This app helps you identify these one-way relationships.

Example:
  • You follow @example_user
  • @example_user doesn't follow you
  • = @example_user is a "non-follower"
This helps you clean up your following list and focus on mutual connections.

Unfollowing Process:
  1. Select users you want to unfollow using the checkboxes
  2. Click the "Unfollow Selected" button
  3. Instagram profiles will open in new tabs
  4. Click the "Following" button on each profile
  5. Confirm the unfollow action
Safety Note: We disabled "Select All" to prevent browser crashes from opening too many tabs at once. Select users in small batches.

Common Issues & Solutions:
File Upload Errors:
  • Make sure files are named correctly (followers_1.json/html, following.json/html)
  • Check that files are under 50MB
  • Ensure you're uploading the right files from your Instagram data export
No Results or Empty Lists:
  • Make sure you uploaded both followers AND following files
  • Check that your Instagram export included follower data
  • Try re-downloading your Instagram data if files seem corrupted
Still having issues? The files you download from Instagram should contain your actual follower and following data in JSON or HTML format.
